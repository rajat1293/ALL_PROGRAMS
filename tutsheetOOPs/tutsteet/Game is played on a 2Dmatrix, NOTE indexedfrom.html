Game is played on a 2D matrix, NOTE: indexed from 1, not from zero Which is of size infinity X zero ( 3 are the no of rows and infinite are the no of columns) student1 starts at element 1,1 student2 starts at element 1,2 student3 starts at element 1,3 RULES of game: a) A student can move only one square down ( increasing i co- ordinate, j co-ordiante remains same) Example : student can move from 2,3 to 3,3 b) if p<q, where p and q are numbers, then student p cannot have higher i co-ordinate c) student1 and student2 cannot be more than L distance apart. same is with the student2 and student3. d) at the end of k moves all should end at same position. INPUT: two integers L and k....where 0<=L<=7 and 0<=k<=10^9 output: how many ways are there to complete the game , modulo 1000000007 Example Input: 1 3 Output: 1 Input: 2 6 Output: 5
